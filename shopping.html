Shopping websites

Whether you want to buy something for yourself because it's your birthday or Christmas, or because the item you want is not available in stores, these websites can help. They sell almost anything and everything.

Amazon

Amazon offers a lot of products that you can't seem to find in stores anywhere. If you can't find it at stores, you may find it here.

Amazon

Ebay

Ebay ot only sells new things, but they also sell used and discontinued things. The items can be very expensive, but it is worth it.

Ebay

Craigslist

This site sells used stuff. The prices are much cheaper than Ebay. The sellers usually want you to meet up with them if you are interested buying stuff from them so safety is important when you use this site to buy things.

Craigslist

Offerup

This site is very similar to Craigslist. It sells used stuff, too. It is not as popular than Craigslist, though.
