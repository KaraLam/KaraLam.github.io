Ideas

Do you like to decorate, draw, write, etc, but have no idea on what to make? Have you ran out of ideas? Looking for something to do that doesn't require the internet? These sites will give you ideas.

Instagram

This site is very popular for users to share ideas on decorating, crafts, etc with other users. Even people without an account can search for ideas.

Instagram

Pinterest

This site gives people lots of ideas, but the downside is that it can not be viewed by anybody and everybody. It requires you to have an account or sign in with Google in order to view them.

Life Hacks

NOTE: You can find life hacks everywhere on the internet, including YouTube. It it not just limited to one particular website, so there is no need to include any particular link. Just Google search "life hacks" and you will get plenty if them.
